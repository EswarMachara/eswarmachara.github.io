Research

Focused on advancing medical image analysis through computer vision and vision-language models, with applications in segmentation, grounding, and diagnostic automation.

Research Interests

Medical Image Analysis

Developing deep learning methods for automated analysis of medical imaging modalities including echocardiography, histopathology, and satellite-based damage assessment. Focus on segmentation architectures and multi-task learning.

Computer Vision

Research in object detection, semantic segmentation, and pose estimation with emphasis on YOLO-based architectures, multi-backbone fusion strategies, and real-time inference optimization for deployment scenarios.

Vision-Language Models

Exploring multi-modal architectures that bridge natural language understanding and visual perception. Current work focuses on referring expression grounding, instruction-conditioned segmentation, and cross-modal alignment.
