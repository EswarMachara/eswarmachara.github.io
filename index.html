About

I am an Undergraduate researcher pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science at Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, Nuzvid, India. My research interests lie at the intersection of deep learning and computer vision, with a deep focus on Medical Imaging, Computer Vision, and Vision-Language Models. My research is driven by a desire to solve real-world clinical and visual understanding problems through domain-adaptive learning.

My work spans cardiac image analysis, histopathology segmentation, referring expression grounding, and underwater debris detection. I have published at ICCV 2025 and have been recognized at national and international competitions including IIT Kharagpur, ISRO, and MAIC South Korea.

I am actively seeking research internships and graduate study opportunities where I can contribute to advancing the state of the art in medical AI and visual reasoning.
